Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026

Prykazka (“we”, “the app”) is a free game for guessing Ukrainian proverbs from rebus illustrations. This policy describes what data is processed on your device and how we handle it.

1. Data on your device

The app stores game progress, favorites, achievements, settings, and statistics locally on your device. No account is required. This data stays on your device until you delete the app or reset progress in the game.

2. iCloud (iOS and iPadOS only)

On iPhone and iPad, achievement progress may sync through iCloud if you have iCloud enabled on your device. Sync is optional and controlled by your Apple ID settings. We do not have access to your Apple account.

3. Microphone and speech recognition

If you choose to dictate an answer, the app requests access to the microphone and system speech recognition. Processing happens on your device through Apple’s services; audio is not sent to our servers.

4. Local notifications

You may optionally enable solo-mode reminders. Notifications are scheduled locally on your device; we do not send push messages from a server.

5. Children

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children. All content is curated (Ukrainian proverbs and rebus illustrations).

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this page from time to time. The current version is always available at this URL.
