How to play

  • Party mode: Home → Party → choose 2–6 teams → Start. Pass the phone between teams after each turn.
  • Solo mode: Home → Solo → open “Starter Pack” → guess proverbs from rebus cards.

FAQ

  • Packs labeled “Coming soon” are future content, not broken purchases. Version 1.0 is free with no in-app purchases.
  • Voice dictation is optional; it requires microphone and speech recognition permissions. You can type answers instead.
  • iCloud optionally syncs achievements on iPhone and iPad when iCloud is enabled on your device.
  • Reminders are local notifications for solo mode; configure them in the Solo section.
